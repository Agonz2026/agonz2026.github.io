Lawyer Mind, Leader Energy

Read the room. Navigate your energy. Lead the moment.

Every table pulls a default energy out of you — and it's rarely the one the moment needs. W.I.N. is the pause button between reaction and intention: Witness the energy, Interpret the moment, Navigate the shift. Pick who's across the table and walk it through.

W Witness — what energy am I bringing? what's across from me? I Interpret — is it helping or hurting? what does this need? N Navigate — choose the energy that serves the outcome.
How to use it

Reach for this in the ninety seconds before you pick up the phone, hit reply, or walk into a room — not after. Pick who's across the table, then run W.I.N.: Witness the energy you're bringing and the one across from you, Interpret whether it's helping or hurting and what the moment needs, and Navigate the shift to the energy that serves the outcome. You don't have to match the energy across the table — you regulate your own instead of absorbing theirs.
